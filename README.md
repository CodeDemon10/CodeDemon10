### Did you know?

The first blockchain was conceptualized in 1991 by Stuart Haber and W. Scott Stornetta, who wanted to create a system where document timestamps could not be tampered with. However, it wasn't until 2008 that Satoshi Nakamoto brought blockchain into the mainstream with Bitcoin!

üîó **Fun Blockchain Facts:**
- The Bitcoin Genesis Block was mined on January 3, 2009
- Ethereum introduced smart contracts in 2015
- The term "Web3" was coined by Ethereum co-founder Gavin Wood in 2014
- NFTs have existed since 2014 (Quantum by Kevin McCoy)
- The first real-world Bitcoin transaction was for 2 pizzas worth 10,000 BTC!
